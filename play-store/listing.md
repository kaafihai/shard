# Baajit — Play Store Listing

## App Name
Baajit — ADHD Task Manager

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Task manager built for ADHD brains. Track habits, moods, and energy your way.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Baajit is a task and habit management app designed from the ground up for people with ADHD. Instead of forcing you into rigid systems that don't stick, Baajit works with the way your brain actually operates — with energy-aware suggestions, gentle transitions between tasks, and a companion rabbit that grows alongside your progress.

YOUR TASKS, YOUR ENERGY
Create tasks with due dates and tag them by energy level. On low-energy days, Baajit surfaces lighter tasks instead of overwhelming you with your entire list. Break big tasks into bite-sized mini-steps when something feels too large to start — your rabbit companion coaches you through the process, and each step becomes its own trackable task.

HABITS THAT ACTUALLY STICK
Build habits with flexible scheduling — daily, specific weekdays, or custom patterns. Track your streaks with calendar heatmaps and detailed stats. Baajit doesn't shame you for missing a day. It just helps you pick back up.

MOOD AND ENERGY CHECK-INS
Log your mood across five levels with optional notes. Check in on your energy each day — from Depleted to Supercharged — and watch how the app adapts to where you are right now, not where you think you should be.

FOCUS WITHOUT THE FUSS
A clean, distraction-free timer with preset durations (5, 15, 25, or 45 minutes). No complicated Pomodoro configurations. Pick a time, press start, earn XP when you finish.

BRAIN DUMP
Racing thoughts? Get them out of your head and into a safe space. Tag your dumps by category and convert the ones that matter into actionable tasks later.

GROUNDING WHEN YOU NEED IT
Three sensory exercises for moments when focus slips: guided box breathing with a visual progress ring, the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique, and a quick body scan. Always one tap away.

TRANSITION PROMPTS
One of the hardest parts of ADHD is switching between tasks without getting stuck. Baajit shows gentle messages from your rabbit between completed items, helping you move forward instead of freezing.

GROW YOUR RABBIT
Everything you do in Baajit earns XP — completing tasks, logging habits, tracking moods, finishing focus sessions, brain dumps. Your rabbit companion grows from a tiny kit to a majestic elder across five levels, unlocking outfits along the way. It's a small thing, but it makes showing up feel worth it.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Baajit uses a local SQLite database — your tasks, moods, and habits never leave your phone.

CALENDAR VIEW
See your tasks, habits, and mood entries together in day and week views. Tap any day to review what happened or plan what's ahead.

Built with care by someone who gets it.
