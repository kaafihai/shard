# Privacy Policy for Baajit

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

## Overview

Baajit is a task and habit management app built for ADHD brains. Your privacy is fundamental to how Baajit works — the app is designed to keep all your data on your device and under your control.

## Data Collection

Baajit does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

- **No account required.** You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in to use Baajit.
- **No analytics or tracking.** Baajit does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or advertising SDKs.
- **No network requests.** Baajit does not connect to the internet. All functionality works entirely offline.
- **No third-party services.** Baajit does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

## Data Storage

All data you enter into Baajit — including tasks, habits, mood logs, focus sessions, brain dumps, energy check-ins, and rabbit progression — is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. This data never leaves your device.

Some lightweight preferences (such as whether you have completed the welcome screen and your current energy level) are stored in your device's local storage.

## Data Deletion

You can delete any individual task, habit, or mood entry from within the app. Uninstalling Baajit removes all app data from your device, including the local database and all stored preferences.

## Children's Privacy

Baajit does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect information from anyone — all data remains on-device.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Since Baajit does not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.
